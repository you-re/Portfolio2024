Little Lion

About

Little Lion was a collaboration with graphic designer Neža Reberc. Initially a graphic design project, it evolved into a small marketing pitch for a product box containing items for newborns. Neža Reberc developed the visual concept, look, and textures, while I handled 3D modeling, texturing, simulations, rendering, and compositing.

Gallery

Tools Used

- Blender: Modeling, Lighting, Animation, Rendering
- Substance Painter: Texturing
- Houdini: Cloth Simulaion
- DaVinci Fusion: Compositing, Color Grading
